Selamat Datang Di Grand Noesantara Hotel

Grand Noesantara Hotel adalah hotel bintang lima yang terletak di bali, hotel ini sering menjadi tujuan para turis ataupun penduduk indonesia yang sedang berlibur di bali untuk beristirahat. ketika sampai di hotel ini anda akan di suguhkan oleh pemandangan hotel yang indah dan kamar yang luas dan para pelayan yang baik hati. lalu juga hotel ini memiliki bermacam macam jenis kamar yang bisa di nikmati di kantong anda, dan makanan yang lezat juga jadi alasan kenapa harus memilih hotel ini sebagai tempat istrahat anda setelah lelah. Lokasi hotel ini sangat lah strategis dikarenakan terletak pada di dekat pantai Lovina yang sangat indah."But I must explain to you how all this mistaken idea of denouncing pleasure and praising pain was born and I will give you a complete account of the system, and expound the actual teachings of the great explorer of the truth, the master-builder of human happiness. No one rejects, dislikes, or avoids pleasure itself, because it is pleasure, but because those who do not know how to pursue pleasure rationally encounter consequences that are extremely painful. Nor again is there anyone who loves or pursues or desires to obtain pain of itself, because it is pain, but because occasionally circumstances occur in which toil and pain can procure him some great pleasure. To take a trivial example, which of us ever undertakes laborious physical exercise, except to obtain some advantage from it? But who has any right to find fault with a man who chooses to enjoy a pleasure that has no annoying consequences, or one who avoids a pain that produces no resultant pleasure?"
